ABOUT ME

With a diverse background in multilevel residential design, commercial, and retail architecture, I am an architect and interior designer eager to explore new challenges and expand my expertise in creating innovative, impactful spaces.

My experience includes designing and constructing apartments and multi-level homes in India, as well as developing food retail kiosks within prominent airports across the USA, including projects at DCA, MIA and DIA airports.

I specialize in interior design for STR properties, conducting market and design trend research to create spaces that attract guests and optimize profitability. I handle everything from mood boards and furniture selection to expense reporting and on-site installations.
