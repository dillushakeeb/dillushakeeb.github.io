Project Overview

A comprehensive portfolio of design and project management work for Halfmoon Empanadas across multiple airport locations in the United States. These projects involved conceptual layout design, massing, equipment selection, 3D modeling, signage design, and overseeing the construction phase as project manager. The kiosks were designed to attract travelers in busy airport environments while optimizing operations in a high-volume, fast-paced setting.

Design Approach

The design approach for Halfmoon Empanadas focused on creating vibrant, eye-catching kiosks that would attract travelers in busy airport environments. The bold brand colors and distinctive geometric patterns were integrated into each design to ensure brand recognition while adapting to the specific requirements and constraints of each airport location. The layouts were optimized for operational efficiency in high-volume, fast-paced settings, with careful consideration given to customer flow, staff movement, and equipment placement.

Washington DCA

The Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport location features a prominent kiosk design with vibrant brand colors and strategic placement to capture traveler attention. The design incorporates efficient service areas, display cases, and preparation space within a limited footprint, maximizing operational efficiency while maintaining visual appeal.

Denver DEN

The Denver International Airport location incorporates a unique bike-themed design element while maintaining brand consistency. This location was designed to accommodate higher traffic volumes with an expanded preparation area and service counter. The distinctive branding and layout ensure visibility in the busy concourse environment.

Miami MIA

The Miami International Airport location involved the renovation of an existing kiosk to align with updated brand standards while improving operational efficiency. The design preserved valuable aspects of the original location while introducing contemporary elements to refresh the customer experience.
