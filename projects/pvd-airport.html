Food & Beverage Program

Rhode Island T.F. Green International Airport

Project Overview

Designed and implemented a comprehensive food and beverage program for Rhode Island T.F. Green International Airport, focusing on showcasing New England's culinary heritage while providing efficient service for travelers. This project required thoughtful space planning across the terminal to create diverse dining options that would appeal to a wide range of travelers.

Design Approach

Our design strategy centered on creating authentic New England experiences through carefully curated food concepts, from traditional seafood to contemporary regional cuisine. The aesthetic incorporated coastal New England elements, with nautical-inspired design details, classic materials, and a color palette that evoked the region's maritime heritage, creating spaces that felt simultaneously contemporary and rooted in local tradition.

Implementation Strategy

Implementation included close collaboration with local food artisans and restaurant operators to create authentic concepts that could function efficiently in an airport environment. We established a multi-phased rollout to maintain operations during renovations and conducted extensive staff training to ensure consistent quality across all venues. The program also included integrating digital ordering technology to improve service speed during peak travel periods.

Project Specifications

  • Client: Rhode Island Airport Corporation
  • Location: Warwick, Rhode Island
  • Year: 2022
  • Size: Multiple locations throughout terminal
  • Project Type: Commercial / F&B
  • Services: Space Planning, Concept Development, Design Coordination
  • Software Used: AutoCAD, SketchUp, Adobe Creative Suite
