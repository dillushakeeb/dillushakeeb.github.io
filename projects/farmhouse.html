Farmhouse G+1 A-Frame

Forest Hill Estate, Bethamangalam, Tamilnadu

Project Overview

The objective of this project was to craft a compact yet cozy rustic cabin using an A-frame structure. As the lead designer, I orchestrated site studies, conceptual layouts, massing refinements, and detailed 3D renderings. Additionally, I contributed to landscaping efforts and the design of the surrounding farmland, ensuring harmony between the cabin and its natural environment.

Design Concept

The A-frame design was chosen for its efficiency, iconic silhouette, and structural integrity. The steep pitch of the roof allows for snow runoff while creating a dramatic interior space with high ceilings. The design emphasizes a strong connection to the outdoors with large glass facades that invite natural light and provide panoramic views of the surrounding forest estate.

The interior layout maximizes the available space despite the triangular constraints of the A-frame structure. The ground floor features an open concept living, dining, and kitchen area, while the upper level contains sleeping quarters accessed via a space-efficient staircase. The design incorporates natural materials throughout, including exposed wood beams and stone elements, to reinforce the rustic cabin aesthetic.

Key Features

  • Classic A-frame architectural design with modern touches
  • Two-level structure with efficient space utilization
  • Large glass front façade for maximum natural light and views
  • Integration with surrounding landscape
  • Natural materials palette including wood and stone
  • Rustic yet comfortable aesthetic suitable for vacation living
  • Energy-efficient design considerations
