Food & Beverage Program

Albuquerque International Sunport, New Mexico
Scroll

Project Overview

Led the comprehensive development of food and beverage program for Albuquerque International Sunport. This project involved space planning, concept development, and coordination with multiple stakeholders to create a cohesive dining experience throughout the airport terminals.

Design Approach

The design approach centered on showcasing local New Mexican culinary culture while ensuring operational efficiency in the airport environment. We focused on creating distinctive venues that would appeal to travelers while highlighting authentic local flavors and incorporating design elements that reflected the region's rich cultural heritage.

Implementation Strategy

Implementation involved close collaboration with airport authorities, food service operators, and construction teams. The strategy included phased construction to minimize disruption to airport operations, careful vendor selection to ensure quality offerings, and comprehensive training programs for staff to maintain consistent service standards across all food and beverage outlets.

Project Specifications

  • Client: Albuquerque International Sunport
  • Location: Albuquerque, New Mexico
  • Year: 2022
  • Size: Multiple locations across terminals
  • Project Type: Commercial / F&B
  • Services: Space Planning, Concept Development, Design Coordination
  • Software Used: AutoCAD, SketchUp, Adobe Creative Suite
