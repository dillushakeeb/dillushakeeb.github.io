Short-Term Rental Properties

Interior Design for STR Properties

Project Overview

I specialize in interior design for Short-Term Rental (STR) properties, conducting market and design trend research to create spaces that attract guests and optimize profitability. My comprehensive approach covers everything from mood boards and furniture selection to expense reporting and on-site installations.

Design Process

My design process for STR properties involves thorough market research to understand local preferences and guest expectations. I create mood boards to visualize concepts and engage clients in the design journey. Each property is approached with a unique perspective, balancing aesthetic appeal with practical functionality for short-term guests.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conducting market research on STR properties to align designs with local market demands and client expectations
  • Researching design trends and creating mood boards to visualize concepts for client approval
  • Selecting furniture, décor, and materials, and preparing detailed expense reports to manage budgets
  • Overseeing end-to-end project management, including hiring and coordinating contractors
  • Traveling to project sites to lead and execute installations, including assembling furniture, painting murals, and finalizing all home elements
  • Managing fast-paced project timelines, completing design and installation within 15 days
  • Conducting post-project reviews and analyzing expense reports to assess property success

Project Specifications

  • Client: BNB Leverage
  • Period: DEC 2024 - CURRENT
  • Project Type: Short-Term Rental Properties
  • Services: Interior Design, Project Management, Installation
  • Typical Timeline: 15 days design to installation
  • On-site Installation: 7-10 days

Design Philosophy

My approach to STR property design focuses on creating spaces that not only photograph well for listings but also provide genuine comfort and functionality for guests. I balance eye-catching design elements with practical considerations for short-term stays.

Each property is designed with a unique character that reflects its location and target audience, while maintaining high standards for durability and ease of maintenance. The goal is to create memorable spaces that generate positive reviews and repeat bookings, ultimately maximizing the property's revenue potential.

Project Success Metrics

Guest Satisfaction: Positive reviews, repeat bookings

Booking Rates: Higher occupancy percentage

Photography Appeal: Standout listing photos

Budget Adherence: Maximizing impact within client budget

Installation Timeline: On-time completion
