Half Moon Empanadas

Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport, Rm B2-606 & B1-673A STORAGE, Bldg 12201

Project Overview

Engaged in conceptual layout and massing for the kiosk, selected equipment, and conducted detailed 3D modeling. Additionally, contributed to signage and graphics design. Transitioned to project management during the construction phase, overseeing vendor selection for millwork, signage, equipment procurement, IT, and low-voltage data. Led the bidding process for general contractors, orchestrated weekly project meetings with all vendors, ensuring schedule adherence.

Design Approach

The design approach focused on creating a vibrant, eye-catching kiosk that would attract travelers in the busy airport environment. The bold pink color scheme and geometric patterns were chosen to make the brand stand out, while the layout was optimized for efficiency in a high-volume, fast-paced setting.

Construction Management

As the project manager during construction, I coordinated between multiple vendors including millwork fabricators, signage manufacturers, equipment suppliers, IT specialists, and general contractors. Weekly progress meetings were established to ensure all parties were aligned and that the project remained on schedule. I managed the bidding process for selecting the general contractor, evaluating proposals based on cost, timeline, and quality of work.
