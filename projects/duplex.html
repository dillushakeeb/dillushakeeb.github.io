G+1 Duplex

Blue Aster, Electronic city, Bangalore

Project Overview

Contributed to zoning and layout planning, as well as the preparation of detailed working drawings including plans, sections, elevations, RCPs, electrical, and plumbing drawings. Additionally, played a key role in designing railing details, fencing, gates, toilet specifications, flooring, and staircase elements. Led the design efforts for elevations and 3D renderings.

Design Approach

This duplex design features a contemporary aesthetic with clean lines and a thoughtful layout that maximizes space utility. The design incorporates a mix of materials including stone cladding and large glass windows, creating a striking visual balance between solidity and openness. Special attention was given to the natural light flow throughout the spaces, with strategic window placements enhancing the interior ambiance.

Key Features

  • Modern architectural design with clean, geometric lines
  • Multi-level layout with optimized spatial planning
  • Custom-designed railings and staircases for unique character
  • Thoughtful integration of outdoor spaces
  • Detailed electrical and plumbing layouts for practical living
  • Elegant elevation design with a mix of materials
